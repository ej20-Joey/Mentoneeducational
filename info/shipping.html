Shipping, Returns & Cancellations

Shipping

Free standard shipping on all orders over $150 (Australia only). Standard delivery 2–5 business days. Express 1–2 business days (additional charge applies). International shipping available — please contact us for a quote.

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in original, unused condition. Please contact us at sales@mentone-educational.com.au before returning any item. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty.

Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled before dispatch. Once dispatched, the returns policy applies. Special order and custom items cannot be cancelled once placed.
