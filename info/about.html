About Mentone Educational

Supplying Australia's medical educators and healthcare professionals since 1979.

Our Story

Mentone Educational started selling medical teaching aids in 1979 and now stocks the largest range of Anatomical Models, Medical Simulation and Health Education products in Australia.

We import from over 10 countries globally and supply tertiary education, schools, hospitals and all healthcare professionals across Australasia. What makes us stand out is the personal service we provide — each member of staff is responsible for a particular product range, ensuring excellent product knowledge.
