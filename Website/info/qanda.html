Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, products, and more.

Ordering & Accounts

Do I need an account to place an order?
No — you can check out as a guest. Creating an account lets you track orders, view your order history, and save your details for a faster checkout next time.
Can I order over the phone?
Yes. Call us on (03) 9547 6638 during business hours (Mon–Fri 9am–5pm AEST) and our team will process your order directly.
Do you offer purchase orders for institutions?
Yes. Schools, universities, hospitals and government departments can place orders by purchase order. Please email sales@mentone-educational.com.au with your PO and billing details.
Are your prices inclusive of GST?
All prices displayed on the website are inclusive of GST. Your tax invoice, issued at the time of purchase, will show the GST component separately.

Shipping & Delivery

How much does shipping cost?
Standard shipping is free on all orders over $150 (Australia only). For orders under $150, a flat rate applies at checkout. Express shipping is available for an additional charge.
How long will my order take to arrive?
Standard delivery takes 2–5 business days. Express delivery takes 1–2 business days. Orders placed before 12pm AEST on business days are typically dispatched the same day.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to New Zealand and select international destinations. Please contact us for a shipping quote before placing your order, as international freight costs vary by location and order size.
Can I track my order?
Yes. Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can also view tracking details in your account under Order History.

Products & Stock

Are all products shown on the website in stock?
We aim to keep stock levels accurate, but availability can change. If a product is out of stock or on back-order, we will contact you promptly to discuss alternatives or estimated lead times.
Can I request a product that isn't listed on your website?
Absolutely. We import from suppliers across more than 10 countries and can often source products not listed online. Contact our team with the product details and we'll do our best to help.
Do you offer product demonstrations?
Yes, for larger simulation products we can arrange demonstrations at our Melbourne showroom or at your institution. Please contact us to organise a suitable time.
Are your products suitable for healthcare professionals?
Yes. We supply hospitals, universities, medical clinics, nursing schools and all healthcare professionals. Some products are gated to verified healthcare or education purchasers — you'll be prompted to confirm your professional status at checkout where applicable.

Returns & Refunds

What is your returns policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in original, unused condition. Please contact us before returning any item. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item is faulty or incorrectly sent.
What if my item arrives damaged or faulty?
Please contact us within 7 days of receiving a damaged or faulty item. Send photos of the damage to sales@mentone-educational.com.au and we'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled before dispatch. Once an order has been dispatched, our standard returns policy applies. Special orders and custom items cannot be cancelled once placed.

Our team is happy to help — get in touch and we'll get back to you quickly.
